## Kill criteria for the launch

- 30 days, 0 paid customers, < 5% reply rate on outbound after one creative rotation → pause outbound and write a teardown post asking peers what to fix.
- 60 days, 0 paid customers → reconsider the wedge entirely (pivot to a single-vertical play).
- 90 days, < 3 active customers → shut the landing and merge findings into a different opportunity.

These thresholds are designed to fail fast so the team doesn't sink a quarter into a false-positive wedge.
