## ICP (ideal customer profile)

A salon, clinic, school, brokerage, or small e-commerce shop with:

- **Inbound DM volume.** ≥ 30 messages/day across messengers.
- **Channel mix.** At least one of Telegram or WhatsApp Business already in use.
- **Knowledge base raw material.** A website, a Notion page, a Google Doc, or a price-list PDF that we can ingest.
- **Owner is the buyer.** No procurement department; budget authority is the owner.
- **Currency.** Comfortable invoicing in USD or EUR (or accepting USDT/USDC via NOWPayments in Wave 2's primary motion).

## Anti-personas (we will not sell to)

- **Junior researchers shopping cheap on Upwork.** Asking for a $50 bot. Walk.
- **Enterprise procurement-led buyers.** Asking for an SOC2 letter, a 30-day pilot agreement, and a master services agreement before the kickoff call. Refer to Decagon / Sierra / Ada.
- **Customers in compliance-sensitive verticals.** Healthcare diagnosis, legal advice, financial advice. Excluded by terms of service.
- **Customers asking for outbound cold messaging on WhatsApp.** Reputational and legal trap. Walk.
- **Customers expecting human chat coverage 24/7.** That's a BPO, not Relayhouse.

## Trust signals the audience checks

- A real day-by-day delivery schedule on the landing page (not "fast turnaround").
- A refund clause stated in a single sentence.
- Founder photo + first name + brief operator history. No fake faces.
- A working language switcher on the landing (RU / ES / EN). Wave 2 ships in English; RU/ES copy is queued for the polish pass.
- A real testimonial before launch month is fine to omit — better to leave the slot empty than fake it.
