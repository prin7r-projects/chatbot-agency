## 2. The problem

- 30-200 inbound DMs/day at a small clinic, salon, school, or shop.
- 20-40% of late-night DMs churn before the human reply.
- One missed booking on a Saturday night = $80-300 lost forever.
- Owner triages on her phone past midnight.

The owner-operator is the bottleneck. She tried ManyChat. She tried Tidio. They handed her a flow canvas. She bounced.

- GLM 5.1 Flash + Claude 4.5 Haiku route makes per-message cost ≈ $0.001 — finally below the SMB price point in cheap currencies.
- The 2017-2019 Messenger flow agencies died. The 2023 GPT-wrapper shops priced themselves out. The owner-operator has no current option.

**A done-for-you chatbot, delivered in 5 working days, on Telegram + WhatsApp + Discord, from one knowledge base.**

- Day 1 — Kickoff + KB ingest.

Not a builder. Not a flow canvas. Not a platform.

## 5. The wedge

Four things no one in our market combines:

1. **Done-for-you delivery in 5 days** — vs ManyChat (DIY) and vs Decagon / Sierra (six-week enterprise sales).
2. **Real LLM understanding on cheap models** — GLM 5.1 Flash + Haiku route. SMB-affordable in RU, LATAM, SEA.
3. **Three channels from one knowledge base** — Telegram, WhatsApp, Discord, plus optional web widget. One bill.
4. **Ongoing tuning subscription** — weekly call, monthly accuracy report. The bot stays accurate; static flows rot.
