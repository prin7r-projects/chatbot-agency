The problem02 / 10

Late-night DMs eat the booking.

A salon, clinic, school, or shop gets 30-200 inbound DMs a day across messengers. Twenty to forty percent of overnight DMs churn before the owner replies — that is the booking.

  • One missed Saturday booking — $80-300 lost, sometimes forever.
  • The owner triages on her phone past midnight.
  • She tried ManyChat, set up two flows, abandoned them.
  • She does not want a builder. She wants a bot.
The owner-operator is the bottleneck02
Why now03 / 10

Three things turned over in the last 18 months.

  • WhatsApp Cloud API is finally self-serve in target geographies.
  • GLM 5.1 Flash + Claude 4.5 Haiku routing pulls per-message cost down to ~$0.001 — finally below SMB unit economics in cheap currencies.
  • The 2017-2019 Messenger flow agencies died. The 2023 GPT-wrapper shops priced themselves out. The owner-operator has no current option.

The market is not new. The pricing footprint is.
